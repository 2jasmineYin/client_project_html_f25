Build Your Professional Network

Networking is one of the most powerful tools for career success. At UMSI, we help you develop the skills and confidence to build meaningful professional relationships that can open doors to opportunities, mentorship, and career growth. Whether you're an introvert or extrovert, networking is a skill that can be learned and mastered.

Why Networking Matters

  • Hidden Job Market: 70-80% of jobs are never posted publicly
  • Industry Insights: Learn about trends, challenges, and opportunities in your field
  • Mentorship Opportunities: Connect with experienced professionals who can guide your career
  • Skill Development: Practice communication and relationship-building skills
  • Long-term Career Growth: Build relationships that will support you throughout your career

Networking Strategies

Online Networking

  • LinkedIn: Optimize your profile and engage with industry content
  • Professional Groups: Join relevant LinkedIn groups and participate in discussions
  • Virtual Events: Attend webinars, conferences, and online meetups
  • Social Media: Follow industry leaders and engage thoughtfully with their content

In-Person Networking

  • Career Fairs: Prepare your elevator pitch and bring business cards
  • Professional Conferences: Attend sessions and networking breaks
  • Alumni Events: Connect with UMSI graduates in your field
  • Industry Meetups: Join local professional organizations

Informational Interviews

Informational interviews are one of the most effective networking tools. They allow you to learn about careers, companies, and industries while building relationships with professionals.

The TIARA Method

  • T - Thank: Express gratitude for their time and insights
  • I - Inquire: Ask thoughtful questions about their career path
  • A - Ask: Request specific advice for your situation
  • R - Reciprocate: Offer to help them in return
  • A - Appreciate: Follow up with a thank you note
